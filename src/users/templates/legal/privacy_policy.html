Privacy Policy

Effective as of April 10, 2025

Issak ("we", "us", or "our") provides an AI-powered web platform that enables users to generate summaries, flashcards, conversational responses, and mind maps from YouTube videos, PDF files, and web content. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you interact with our website, services. (collectively, the “Service”).

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how your data is handled.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Content Data: Text, video URLs, and documents you upload to our platform for processing.
  • User-Generated Input: Prompts, questions, summaries, and other interactions with our AI services.
  • Account Information: Name, email address, login credentials (if registration is enabled).
  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, interaction history, and session data.
  • Third-Party Integrations: Information from connected services like Notion or Telegram, with your consent.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Analyze content using AI for summaries, flashcards, chats, and mind maps.
  • Personalize your experience on the platform.
  • Communicate updates, support responses, and account notices.
  • Train our models using anonymized data (unless otherwise noted).

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your data. We may share it with:

  • Service providers for hosting, analytics, and support.
  • Telegram, Notion, or other third parties only when you initiate integration.
  • Authorities or acquirers in compliance with the law or business transfer.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Track usage analytics,
  • Remember session preferences,
  • Improve overall user experience.

You may manage cookies via browser settings.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose it was collected for, or to comply with legal obligations. Uploaded files and prompt data may be temporarily cached and deleted after processing unless saved to your account.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your data
  • Export your content
  • Withdraw previously given consent

7. Security

We use industry-standard methods to protect your data but cannot guarantee complete security. Use strong passwords and be cautious when sharing sensitive information.

8. Children’s Privacy

This service is not intended for children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. International Users & GDPR

If you are located in Europe:

  • Your data may be processed outside the EEA/UK with appropriate safeguards.
  • We rely on lawful bases such as Consent, Contractual Necessity, or Legitimate Interest.
  • You have the right to object, restrict, access, correct, or delete your data under GDPR.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be published on this page with the effective date noted above.
